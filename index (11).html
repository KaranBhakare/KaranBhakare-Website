My name is Karan Bhakare and I am a prospective medical student in Cardiology. I graduated from the University of California, Riverside in 2023 with a degree in Biology. My experience includes 4 years as an undergraduate reseraacher in the Fiacco Lab. I have worked in the Fiacco Lab to study glial cells and interactions between astrocytes and volume regulated mechanisms in the brain. I have also given presentations on my research summary in undergraduate research thorugh a program called RISE. This summer program was a 10 week long research project supervised by the Genomics and Dynamic Genome Team on UCR campus in Campbell Hall. In this laboratory experience, I learned how the techniques of gel electrophoresis, bacterial transformation, DNA extraction, and primer design. I also worked alongside researchers from Keck Graduate Institute.

I have experience in health care fields as a Medical Scribe at Loma Linda University Medical Center. I work alongside emergency personnel to organize workflow and patient care for Doctors. I am responsible for charting each patient's history in the Emergency Department in a method which increases efficiency for the Doctors and gives them more time for patient care. As an employee of Loma Linda University Medical Center, I have worked with many emergency Attendings and Medical Residents. I am currently enrolling in EMT classes to become a part-time EMT.

I have also volunteered as a COPE Health Scholar at Riverside Community Hospital. This experience showed me different floors in the hospital. Every 3 months, I would rotate to a new floor/speciality of the hospital. For example, one rotation could be in the Emergency Department and the next rotation would tak e me to the Operating Room. This experience was an integral aspect of my interest in Medicine. My interest in Cardiology comes from my rotation in the Cardiac Catheter Lab where I saw numerous procedures done on patients with cardiac problems. One procedure I observed was a surgical thrombectomy which is a procedure to remove clotting in the bloodstream. A very serious condition called Deep Vein Thrombosis causes clotting in the blood vessels. A surgical thrombectomy is performed by making a cut in the artery and removing the clot and applying a stent. I plan on applying to medical school after graduating and completing a Masters in Medical Sciences. In the future, I wish to become a Cardiothoracic Surgeon. My other hobbies include dancing, playing tennis and basketball, and reading. I particpated on an all boys dance team called UCR AAG where we specialized in Bollywood and Hip-Hop Dancing.
